Recipe

Below you will find a recipe for the world’s best brownie. Follow the directions closely and you will be very satisfied with the result!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
    • Fruit sugar or baker’s special sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • Sea salt if possible.
  • 1/2 cup flour
    • Whole wheat flour is also an option.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix oil and sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla and blend well.
  4. Mix all dry ingredients in a seperate bowl.
  5. Stir dry ingredients into the oil/sugar mixture.
  6. Pour into greased 9x9 square pan.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.
  8. Cool completely before cutting.
